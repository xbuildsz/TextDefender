Why is it safe?

  • Your password isn't sent to our server - we can't decrypt your text even if we wanted to.
    Only encrypted text is sent over the Internet.
  • No cookies, no sessions, no registration, no users tracking.
    We can't identify you at all. We can't know when the same user visits two different sites.
  • No logging in or out, just close the browser tab and you're safe.
  • No ads - we hate ads because they can track you, and they're distracting.
  • You don't have to trust anyone or agree on anything - check the code yourself.
    All our code is well written and full of comments so that you can understand it in details - start by looking at main.js, and check communication with our servers.
  • If someone wants your text, he'll need your password, but he'll have to find your site first.
    Overwrite protection
  • You can use the same sub-site from multiple browsers/devices at the same time.

Your privacy is our priority. Your data is not shared with or accessible by developers. Enjoy using Text Defender for all your text management needs!
